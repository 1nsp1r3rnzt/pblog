## Core Concept

The power of 1% improvements compounded over time.

## Four Laws of Behavior Change

1. **Make it Obvious** - Design your environment
2. **Make it Attractive** - Use temptation bundling  
3. **Make it Easy** - Reduce friction
4. **Make it Satisfying** - Immediate rewards

## Personal Applications

- Morning routine habit stacking
- Environment design for better choices
- 2-minute rule for new habits
